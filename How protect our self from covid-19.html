To prevent the spread of Covid-19 disease, do the following:

(1) Keep cleaning your hands.

(2) Use soap and water or an alcohol solution to sterilize the hands.

(3) Keep a safe distance from anyone coughing or sneezing.

(4) Do not touch your eyes, nose, or mouth.

(5) Make sure to cover your mouth and nose by bending the elbow or a tissue with coughing or sneezing.

(6) Go home if you feel sick.

(7) If you have a fever, cough, and trouble breathing, seek medical attention. And call the care facility before you go.

(8) Follow the directions of the local health authorities.
